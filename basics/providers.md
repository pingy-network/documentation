# Providers

Pingy uses a random selection of RPC providers to confirm the validity of your onchain transactions. We are watching your multisig from different locations and require an exact match to guarantee your onchain security.

* [https://www.1rpc.io](https://www.1rpc.io/)
* [https://www.alchemy.com](https://www.alchemy.com/)
* [https://www.allthatnode.com](https://www.allthatnode.com/)
* [https://www.ankr.com](https://www.ankr.com/)
* [https://www.blockdaemon.com](https://www.blockdaemon.com/)
* [https://chainstack.com](https://chainstack.com/)
* [https://www.indexing.co](https://www.indexing.co/)
* [https://www.infura.io](https://www.infura.io/)
* [https://www.quicknode.com](https://www.quicknode.com/)
* Wildcard: reach out to get listed!
